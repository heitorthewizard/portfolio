Who is Heitor?

A modern wizard casting spells with zeros and ones!

Let me tell you a little bit about how I fell in love with software development.
In 2019 I was still in high school and working as a English teacher at the same institution where I studied the language. I was also studying finance, business management and economy for fun. I came to play a little in the stock market following the Warren Buffet's methodology, I was starting to put money to work for me.

I also really wanted to undertake something profitable and that would last for several years, however, I had no idea what to do, I was kind of lost. In 2020, around the same time the pandemic started, a family member gave me a laptop so I could study with better conditions.

So one day I was looking at the computer immersed in my thoughts until I had an epiphany of how amazing a computer is, a machine that you can create whatever you imagine and the single one limit is the hardware, the possibility of creating new worlds, solving problems with automation and innovating got me really excited! And I decided that I was going to learn software development just for fun. I ended up falling in love and losing track of time while studying.

As I've always been considered a creative person and I consider myself an artist, I thought it was the ideal field for me, a mixture of human and exact sciences, it perfectly fit my characteristics and goals. For example, being able to work from anywhere in the world, being able to work while traveling, enjoying life, and being able to create something great with just a computer and internet access, as some people who founded their companies from a garage and even from their own room. A universe of infinite possibilities.

I'm a highly focused guy, proactive, understanding, fun, ambitious and smart. I strongly follow the Stoic philosophy, which helps me to handle life in a lighter and more balanced way. I place thought and action resulting in a common good, which is the stoic virtue of Justice. I free myself from distractions, I only focus on those things that I can control and do everything as if it was my last act, giving the best of myself. I believe that by improving myself, I am improving the world.

Why Heitor The Wizard?

I believe that computers are something magical, an artifact that manipulates energy as we wish, allowing us to express our imagination, where anything is possible. They allow us to interact with others on the other side of the world in real time, record our best moments with loved ones, and also remember the voice and image of those we once loved and who are gone. They make our lives easier on a daily basis, they help debilitated people. They help us to cure diseases, to solve problems that would take years and years to be solved without them. It is the humanity's most important creation. It's unreal how we've taken elements from nature and turn them into this 100% logical "box". Therefore, I believe that the people who manipulate this artifact are actually manipulating energy, creating new universes, programs, applications, games, etc. Amazing works of art. These people to me are just like wizards casting spells. And I'm one of them!
