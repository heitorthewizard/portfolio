Who is Heitor?

A modern wizard casting spells with zeros and ones! 😆

Let me tell you a little bit about the story of how I fell in love with software development.
In 2019 I was still in high school and working at the same time as an English teacher at the same institution where I studied English. I was also studying finance, business management and economy for fun. I even got to play a little bit in the stock market following Warren Buffet's method, I wanted to start putting money to work for me.

I really wanted to undertake something sustainable that would last for many years, but I had no idea what to do, I was quite lost. In 2020, around the same time the pandemic started, a relative gave me a laptop so I could study in a better way.

So one day I was looking at the computer immersed in my thoughts until I had an epiphany of how amazing a computer is, a machine that you can create whatever you imagine and the single one limit is the hardware, the possibility of creating new worlds, solving problems and innovating got me really excited! And I decided that I was going to learn software development just for fun. I ended up falling in love and losing track of time while studying.

As I always thought of myself as a creative person and consider myself an artist, I thought it was the perfect area for me, which completely fit my characteristics and goals. For example, being able to work from anywhere in the world, being able to work while traveling, being able to create something great with just a computer and internet access, among infinite possibilities.

I am highly focused, proactive, understanding, fun, smart and ambitious. I strongly follow the Stoic philosophy which helps me to handle life in a lighter way. I place thought and action resulting in a common good, which is the Stoic virtue of Justice. I free myself from distractions, I only focus on what's in my control and do everything as if it were my my last act.

Why Heitor The Wizard?

I believe computers are something magical, an artifact that manipulates energy as we wish, allowing us to express our imagination, where anything is possible. A magic box which we can interact with other people in another side of the world in real time, record our special moments with our beloved ones and, even remember the voice and image of the ones we once loved who are gone. It's unreal how we've taken elements from nature and turn them into this one hundred percent logical box...
I believe the people who create these universes, games, programs, applications, and all those pieces of art are modern wizards. And I am one of them!! 😎

My Mission

I seek to acquire experience by applying software development to everything I'm working on, bringing innovation, automation, value and problem solving. I'd love to understand how a great company works, how to team up with capable people with whom I can learn from and evolve as much as I can, improving my skills on dealing with people, negotiations, problem solving and software development. I want to leave a legacy of great value wherever I go. Only then I will be able to start my own business that so far I have no idea what is it gonna be.
